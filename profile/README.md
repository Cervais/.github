# Cervais

### Secure the edge. Protect the mission.

Cervais builds cybersecurity for the systems the world can't afford to lose — critical infrastructure, operational technology (OT), and the industrial edge. We help energy, transportation, healthcare, federal, manufacturing, and agriculture organizations keep essential systems safe, compliant, and running, where uptime, safety, and reliability are non‑negotiable.

### How we work

- **Edge‑native, OT‑aware** — security controls designed to respect the constraints of industrial systems, not disrupt them.
- **Zero Trust by default** — MFA, session control, and least‑privilege access across IT, OT, and legacy assets.
- **Audit‑ready** — built to align with NIST, ISO 27001, and IEC 62443.
- **Real‑time** — continuous monitoring, behavior‑based analysis, and automated response.
